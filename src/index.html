About

Become a Zombie and join over 10000 dancers in a world wide flash mob. Use our app to learn the dance moves to Michael Jackson's Thriller, connect with friends, and post your pictures online. Your prize is to make history as we celebrate the 30th anniversary of Thriller and set out break the Guiness Book of World Records for the largest simultaneous Thriller dance. Roarrrr!

In our mobile app you can:

  • Collect over 20 Signature Michael Jackson Moves
  • Workout to a 6 minute dance routine
  • Get quick 15 second dance lessons
  • Earn points for practicing so you stay motivated
  • Countdown timer to Thrilltime where everyone gathers to beat the world record.

Go from being a novice dancer to feeling like a pro!

Zombies Rise for a good cause.

That'sMyMove! is a San Francisco based mobile product team. We exist to bring the joy of all types of dance and movement to people everywhere. Please follow us @thatsmymove.
